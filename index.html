About Us

VISON MAGITECH further conducts training and workshops in Robotics and Computer Vision, Embedded System, Mechanical Aid Design, IoT(Internet of Things), Human Emotion Recognition, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence and develop its students for real life professional situation.

Our Mission

In our day to day lives we face many problems to which a solution exists but nowadays people are so busy with their lives and jobs that even engineers forget the use of technology to create something productive and unconventional. So with this, Vision MagiTech is established to create magic in the society using Technology. The main Motto of Vision MagiTech is to create some trailblazing products to help the society from various problems that could be easily solved. Vision MagiTech would be soon launching an online platform under the brand name ALLAY in the coming years which would grant a safe, secured and technified society.

Our Vision

Vision Magitech in the upcoming years would be launching some products under the Brand of VISION MAGITECH which would be applicable in all segments of the society like health, education, cleanliness, agriculture, construction etc. The main Vision is to devise new products that would provide protection as well as aid an individual to a hassle free life. In the upcoming years, we plan to provide job opportunities to the young inspiring minds to play with technology and frame themselves into a futuristic TECH generation. Northeast is slowly becoming an IT and Industry Hub. Therefore Vision Magitech aims to expand the technical platform to a great extend and become a part of this revolutionary exhancing IT business.

Research and Development

Research and Development Cell is the backbone of Vision Magitech. Research work is done for the development of Products like Woman Security Products, Life Safety Products, Home Automation, Surveillance Security System, Uses of Robotics and AI Technology in Agriculture, Unmanned Vehicles. The cell is indulged with various collaborative research based projects and finally furnishing those projects into real time products. Vision Magitech involve and engage the minds of various students from different premier institutes to explore their innovative world and create history in the field of invention. The R&D cell holds many eminent experienced reseach scholars and faculties from different esteemed institutes as its Mentors who provide their valuable guidance in the research works being conducted in the cell. The cell holds proper utilization of time and ideas to transform them into real life products that could make work easier and further safeguard the society with the help of technology.
